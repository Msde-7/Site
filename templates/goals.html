Safety

Protecting Our Campus Community

ACTION prioritizes the well-being of every student by addressing critical issues like sexual violence prevention, mental health resources, food and drug safety, and campus infrastructure improvements. From advocating for a streamlined reporting system for survivors to expanding mental health services and improving campus lighting, we are committed to creating a secure, inclusive environment where everyone feels protected and respected.

Community

Building Connections That Matter

We believe a thriving campus begins with a strong sense of belonging. ACTION focuses on fostering unity through events, cultural summits, and mentorship programs that bridge gaps between students of all backgrounds. By amplifying student voices, collaborating with local businesses, and creating platforms for engagement, we aim to strengthen the bonds that make Indiana University a vibrant and diverse community.

Success

Empowering Every Student to Thrive

ACTION is dedicated to providing students with the resources and opportunities they need to excel academically, financially, and personally. From increasing access to affordable tutoring and past syllabi to advocating for fair financial aid policies and streamlined student organization processes, we're paving the way for every Hoosier to achieve their goals and make their mark on campus and beyond.
