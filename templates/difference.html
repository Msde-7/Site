Our Difference

Not Just Another Campaign—A Movement for Real Change

Here's how we stand apart from the current administration and the competing ticket:

Transparency

A Clear and Open Administration

What Sets Us Apart:

The current administration has left many students wondering what student government even does. ACTION will shine a light on every decision, ensuring all processes and initiatives are visible and easy to follow.

Our Promise:

Regular updates, a live budget tracker, and open office hours for students to voice their concerns directly.

Why It Matters:

You deserve to know how your government works and where your student fees go.

Accountability

Promises Kept, Not Forgotten

What Sets Us Apart:

Other campaigns make promises during election season, only to let them fade away. ACTION is built on a foundation of accountability, encouraging students to hold us to our word.

Our Promise:

We'll track and publish the progress of every campaign promise, ensuring the student body knows what's been achieved and what's in progress.

Why It Matters:

Trust is earned through action, not words.

Proactivity

Aggressively Tackling Issues

What Sets Us Apart:

The current administration often reacts to problems after they arise, and the competing ticket lacks a clear plan for proactive change. ACTION will take the lead in addressing issues before they become roadblocks.

Our Promise:

We will push for immediate changes in campus safety, student engagement, and academic resources, guided by ongoing feedback from the student body.

Why It Matters:

A good administration doesn't wait—it takes action to make things better today.

Advocacy

Truly Listening to Students

What Sets Us Apart:

Many students feel unheard under the current administration, and the opposing ticket offers little in terms of student-focused solutions. ACTION is here to amplify your voice.

Our Promise:

Policies built with student input, regular town halls, and online forums to ensure your needs drive our decisions.

Why It Matters:

You deserve a government that works for you, not one that operates behind closed doors.

Innovation

Simplifying Your College Experience

What Sets Us Apart:

ACTION prioritizes efficiency and ease in every initiative, unlike the current administration's overly complex processes and the vague proposals from our opponents.

Our Promise:

From streamlining support services to creating centralized resources for jobs, syllabi, and more, we'll eliminate red tape and make life on campus simpler.

Why It Matters:

College should be about growth and opportunity, not navigating unnecessary hurdles.

Why Choose ACTION?

The choice is clear. While others may promise, ACTION delivers. We're not just running to lead—we're running to serve, improve, and make a tangible difference in your college experience. Together, let's take ACTION.
