Action Starts Here

Building a Better Future for Indiana University Through Student Innovation and Advocacy

Lights. Camera. Action!

Vision

To create a connected and supportive Indiana University community where every student feels valued, protected, and equipped with the tools to excel academically, socially, and personally while championing equity, sustainability, and innovation.

Mission

To foster a safe, inclusive, and thriving campus environment by advocating for student-focused initiatives, enhancing transparency, and empowering every student to succeed through innovative policies and community engagement.

Impact

The ACTION administration focuses on creating a transparent, inclusive, and engaging student government at Indiana University. Their platform is built on three pillars: Safety, Community, and Success, with additional miscellaneous initiatives. These policies aim to enhance campus safety, mental health, inclusivity, academic resources, and sustainability while fostering a strong sense of community and student engagement.

Ava Smith

Vice Presidential Candidate

Hi, I'm Ava Smith from Chatham, NJ! I am a student at Indiana University, majoring in Healthcare Management and Policy with a minor in Human Resources. My passion for leadership and service has shaped my time at IU, and I'm committed to making a difference both on campus and beyond.

O'Neill School Ambassador
As an ambassador for the O'Neill School of Public and Environmental Affairs, I guide prospective students by giving tours, leading information sessions, and supporting their transition to IU.

IUDM Hospital Relations Committee
My involvement with IUDM (Indiana University Dance Marathon) allows me to directly support Riley Hospital for Children, a cause close to my heart.

Alpha Gamma Delta
I'm also a proud member of Alpha Gamma Delta sorority, where I contribute to fighting hunger both nationally and locally through efforts like volunteering at the Hoosier Hills Food Bank in Bloomington.

I believe in the power of collective action to improve lives, and I'm excited to bring that same energy and drive to the ACTION campaign. Together, we can make IU a campus where every voice is heard, every student is supported, and every opportunity is within reach.
