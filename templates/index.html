Action Starts Here

Building a Better Future for Indiana University Through Student Innovation and Advocacy

Lights. Camera. Action!

Vision

To create a connected and supportive Indiana University community where every student feels valued, protected, and equipped with the tools to excel academically, socially, and personally while championing equity, sustainability, and innovation.

Mission

To foster a safe, inclusive, and thriving campus environment by advocating for student-focused initiatives, enhancing transparency, and empowering every student to succeed through innovative policies and community engagement.

Impact

The ACTION administration focuses on creating a transparent, inclusive, and engaging student government at Indiana University. Their platform is built on three pillars: Safety, Community, and Success, with additional miscellaneous initiatives. These policies aim to enhance campus safety, mental health, inclusivity, academic resources, and sustainability while fostering a strong sense of community and student engagement.

Presidential Candidate

Hi, I'm Zachary Goldberg from Long Grove, IL! I am a student at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, pursuing a degree in Finance, Law Ethics & Decision Making, and Business Analytics. My passion for leadership, advocacy, and innovation has defined my experience at IU, and I am committed to driving meaningful change on campus and beyond.

Chief Director of Federal Relations – Indiana University Student Government
In my role with IUSG, I manage relations between IU and federal government entities, advocating for key student initiatives such as Pell Grants, food security, and anti-hazing policies. I work closely with the IU Office of Federal Relations and congressional offices to ensure student voices are heard at the highest levels.

Corporate Law Club – President
As President of the Corporate Law Club, I lead a team of directors to organize engaging events, guest speaker sessions, and discussions on corporate law topics. My goal is to foster a community of students passionate about legal studies and professional development in the legal and business sectors.

Generative AI & LLMs in Business Club – Secretary of Fed
In this role, I help organize events focused on artificial intelligence and machine learning for a growing student community. By leveraging technology and innovative strategies, I work to enhance engagement and awareness of AI's role in the future of business.

I believe in the power of collaboration and bold leadership to create opportunities for all students. Through my work in student government, law, and technology, I am committed to making IU a place where every student's voice matters and every opportunity is within reach.
